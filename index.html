About Our Workshop

Established in 2004, Friends Engineering Works has been at the forefront of industrial fabrication in Karachi, located in the heart of the Korangi Industrial Area.

Our 2,000 sqft facility is equipped with modern welding tools, advanced lathe machines, and state-of-the-art fabrication equipment to handle projects of any scale.

Our team of certified welders and engineers combines traditional craftsmanship with technical expertise to deliver superior quality and reliability.
